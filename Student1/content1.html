  • Physical Health

    Physical health refers to the state of well-being and optimal functioning of the body's physiological systems.

    Good physical health is characterized by the absence of illness or disease and the ability to perform daily activities with ease and energy.

  • Nutrition

    A balanced diet rich in vitamins, minerals, proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats is crucial for maintaining optimal physical health. Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains in your diet.

  • Hydration

    Staying hydrated is essential for regulating body temperature, transporting nutrients, and removing waste products.

    Drinking an adequate amount of water throughout the day helps maintain proper bodily functions.

    Aim for at least 8 glasses (64 ounces) of water daily, or more if you're physically active or in hot weather.

  • sleep

    Quality sleep is vital for physical health as it allows the body to repair and rejuvenate itself.

    Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.

  • Hygiene

    Good personal hygiene practices, such as regular handwashing, dental care, and bathing, are essential for preventing illness and maintaining overall health.
