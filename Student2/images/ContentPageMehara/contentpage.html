Mental Health

"Mental health equals physical health. Emotional, psychological, and social well-being matter. Stress management, therapy, social support, and addressing issues like depression are crucial."

Emotional Well-being

Emotional well-being is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage emotions effectively, contributing to resilience and overall psychological health.

Cognitive Functioning

Cognitive health involves mental processes like thinking, memory, and problem-solving, influenced by genetics, brain health, education, and environment.

Social Relationships

Social well-being relates to quality relationships, effective communication, and supportive networks, fostering belonging and fulfillment.

Behavioral Patterns

Behavioral health involves actions that affect mental well-being, including self-care and recognizing harmful behaviors like substance abuse.
